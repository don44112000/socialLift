# Create a TikTok for Business account

Before you can use TikTok API for Business, you need to sign up at the TikTok for Business Developers portal. Complete the following steps to create a TikTok for Business account.
## Steps
1. Go to [the TikTok for Business Developers homepage](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal) and click the registration button.

![TikTok for Business Developers homepage](https://images.ctfassets.net/5jqet0ab9qci/7wbdK20AnTkjL5N4jPpYZv/fdf20905f7e5f8fb872cdac9c9a13191/TikTok_for_Business_Developers_homepage-EN.png)

2. On the page that pops up, fill in your email address (or phone number), password, check the box to indicate that you agree to the terms, conditions, and privacy policy, and click **Sign Up**. 
- For email, we recommend using an alias such as developers+tiktok@company.com and sharing the login credentials with all the developers on your team after registration to ensure everyone has access to the TikTok for Business account.

![TikTok for Business account signup](https://images.ctfassets.net/5jqet0ab9qci/1qJr2jKz9ztYNRGxEYIi77/019d8aac217f3b0fb9b4b4b370120994/TikTok_for_Business_account_signup-EN.png)

3. Fill in the verification code that is sent to your phone or email. Your account is now created.

![TikTok for Business account signup verification](https://images.ctfassets.net/5jqet0ab9qci/4oEFDIhjj8RvkGgxw2wvy6/47db488fb4918e682a448183916e4ac6/TikTok_for_Business_account_signup_verification-EN.png)
