# Register as a developer

Before you can create an app and start development, you need to register as a developer. Complete the following steps to register as a developer.
# Steps

1. Log in to [TikTok for Business Developers portal](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal). Click **Become a Developer**.

<image src="https://sf16-adcdn-sg.ibytedtos.com/obj/open-api-file-public-i18n/e38a2553324acc568a74e92247796145" alt="Become a Developer" width="80%" style="color:#808080"/>

2. Fill in your name and communication email. For email, we recommend using an alias like <a href = "mailto:developers+tiktok@company.com">developers+tiktok@company.com</a> and sharing the login credentials with all the developers on your team to ensure everyone has access to the developer account.<br>
  Note that communication email must be a verified **company domain** email. You will be rejected if you are using a personal email or a temporary email. In this case, revise your information and re-submit the application.

3. Click **Send Code** to send a verification code to your email address. Copy the verification code and paste it to the Verification Code text field. 

4. Select the user type that best describes your company, and click **Next** to move to the next step. Ensure that the type you selected matches the information you provide, otherwise your profile might be rejected.

	* Technology Company
	* Direct Advertiser
	* Agency

5. Fill in your company name. Your company name should:
	* Be a valid brand name or a legit entity name.
	* Be relevant to the email domain or company website. If it does not match, please provide proof of relevancy in the description section below.

6. Fill in your company website. Your website should:
  	- Be publicly accessible.
 	 - Be valid, functioning, fully developed and professionally presented.
  	- Present relevant business content that is accessible without requiring login, detailing your product and company information.
  	- Match the email domain you have provided.
  	- Be hosted on a domain that is owned by your company. It should not contain domain names associated with social media platforms, third-party hosting companies, e-commerce platforms, etc.
 	 - Be the actual domain of your company website. Shortened links are not accepted. 
  	- Be a company website, rather than a personal website. Currently, we are unable to onboard personal accounts or individual developers. If you are part of a company, please use your company website. 

7. Select your Primary Developer Location. It should be the primary location of the development team in your company, rather than the place where you run your business. For example, if your company is doing business in Singapore, but the developer is based in China, select China in this case.
> <span style="color: DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>For China-outbound developers, please choose China as Primary Developer Location, regardless of the location where your company operates business.

8. Specify other information about your company and businesses, and click **Submit**. Please note these questions will differ depending on the type of developer you are. <br>For the question about how you plan to use TikTok data and API, please be as descriptive as possible, otherwise your profile might be rejected. Your description should explain the use cases selected in the previous question and the purpose of registering as a developer. Here is an example for your reference:
<br>[company name] is a e-commerce company headquartered in [country/region], operating the brand [brand name] and e-commerce website in [country/region]. We are the product and development team, and we develop tools integrating Marketing APIs to support our marketing team.
> <span style="color: DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>You can also use the description field to include additional information you want to specify. 


9. You will be notified of the review result in three business days.
