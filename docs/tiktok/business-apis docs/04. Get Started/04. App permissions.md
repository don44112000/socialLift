# Update app permissions

If you need to make adjustments to your permission, you do not need to create a new developer application or submit a ticket at Developer Ticket Platform. You can update these yourself.

To do this, please follow the steps below:
1. Once logged in, click **My Apps** and click the developer application name you would like to update.
2. Once selected, click **Scope of permission**.
3. Click the edit icon and select the new permissions you would like to apply for.
4. Please fill in the reason why you would like to update your permission and click **Submit**. Ensure that the reasoning provided for the scope increase align with your newly selected scopes. Here is an example for your reference:
 <br>Our campaign managers spend a lot of time uploading creative assets to ad accounts manually. We want to integrate API for creative upload, and produce the capability of automatic creative upload to save campaign managers' time.
5. Your application will now be reviewed by the team. Please note that this process may take between two and three business days.
