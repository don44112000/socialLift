# Need support

If you encounter issues during your integration process, we recommend first referring to our [FAQs](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1740029175352322) or searching for answers in our documentation. If you can't find the solutions you need, please consider the following methods:

- Technical issues: For issues related to integration or other technical difficulties, please **submit a ticket** on our [Developer Ticket Platform](https://ads.tiktok.com/athena/requester/boards?identify_key=b25033ef7782d41050cbac74f751d547e9d8ad35a39a541a0f0efbbd51fe722f). Our Support team is dedicated to addressing your concerns promptly.

- Documentation issues: If you discover issues with our documentation (such as missing, incorrect, or confusing information, a broken link, or a typo), we'd appreciate your feedback. Please click on the **feedback window in the lower right corner** and provide detailed information about the issue.
