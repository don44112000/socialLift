# Create Lead Generation ads

This article walks you through the steps to create Lead Generation ads.  


# Introduction
 Lead Generation is an ad objective through which advertisers can drive sales and generate leads on TikTok. You can create Lead Generation ads to encourage prospects to fill out a form and provide their information, where personal information is pre-filled (for example, Email and Name), to signal their interest in your products or services. 
 
**You can use Campaign Management API to create Lead Generation ads, and this helps streamline your ad creation experience, and elevate operational efficiency and scalability.**

# Prerequisites
- You've gained access to TikTok API for Business. See [Get Started - Step by step workflow](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1735713609895937&rid=7llhcla7zmh) for details. 
  - To create Lead Generation Ads, you need relevant permissions. See [API Reference](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1735713875563521) to find out permissions required for endpoints (including the endpoints listed in the "Steps" section) and see [Update app permissions](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738855280338946&rid=7llhcla7zmh) to find out how to configure permissions.  
  - If you want to create a Lead Generation ad with an Instant Form, an Instant Form is necessary.
