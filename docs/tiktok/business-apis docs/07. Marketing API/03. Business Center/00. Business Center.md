# Business Center

You can use Business Center API to efficiently manage multiple TikTok ad accounts, users, assets, and finances. You can also use Reporting API to run a [Business Center report](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775747432712194).
- To learn about Business Center concepts, benefits and types, see [Overview](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739562432184322).
- To find out some common use cases for Business Center members, partners, assets and payment settings, and Business Center reporting, see [Guides](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739562439582722).
  - [BC creation](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705308039169): Creating a TikTok Business Center will allow you to manage your TikTok ad accounts between multiple users in a secure and centralized environment.
  - [Members and Partners](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705315233793): You can use Business Center API to add people to your Business Center and assign them roles to manage your assets. 
  - [Assets](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705318594562): You can use Business Center API to manage [Assets](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705407307777) or [Asset Groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705410074626) in a Business Center. For example, adding ad accounts or transferring a pixel. 
  - [Finance](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754705321657346): You can use Business Center API to manage payments and invoices in Business Center. For example, you can get the balance of a Business Center or an ad account in the Business Center, or view the transaction records of an ad account or a Business Center.
  - [Reporting](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775798662604802): You can use Reporting API to get data insights into ad accounts in a Business Center.
- To find out what APIs are available for managing Business Center, see [API Reference](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739562438061058).
