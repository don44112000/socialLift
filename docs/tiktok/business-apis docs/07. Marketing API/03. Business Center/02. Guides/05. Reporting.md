# Reporting

Analyzing the cost and performance of ad accounts within a Business Center provides you with centralized and secure management of ad accounts across multiple users. With Business Center reporting, you can efficiently calculate payments for multiple ad accounts, and gain insights to evaluate the effectiveness of your ads. 

To learn about the supported dimensions, metrics, filters and use cases for Business Center reports, refer to [Business Center reports](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775747432712194).
