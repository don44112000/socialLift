# Overview

TikTok Store is an entity or object that allows a merchant to manage and sell products on TikTok. TikTok creators can bind their TikTok account to the store to start selling products using their TikTok account.  <p>
Below are some roles and concepts for TikTok Store. 
- Merchant: A person or company that wants to sell products on TikTok.
- Developer: A person or technical partner that is responsible for integrating or using TikTok Marketing API.
- TikTok business account: A TikTok account type that provides capabilities for businesses to directly promote their products via their own account and get insights into audiences.
- TikTok For Business account: An account that is intended to be used by advertisers or agencies to manage ad campaigns and measure ad performances.
- TikTok API for Business developer application: This is an entity that carries the permissions that a merchant or advertiser grants to a developer, so that the developer can manage their TikTok For Business account on their behalf.
