# Guides

These guides will walk you through the steps to complete common tasks for managing your campaigns, ad groups, and ads.
  - [Campaign](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292231199745): Campaigns are the highest level of advertising. To promote on TikTok for Business, you first need to create a campaign and set your advertising objective.
	- [Create a campaign](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1737719523561474): Introduces how to create a campaign.
	- [Copy a campaign](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1788434533212162): Introduces how to copy a campaign.
	- [Advertising objective](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1737585562434561): Introduces supported advertising objectives for auction ads and Reach & Frequency ads.
	- [Reach & Frequency](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739470723315713): Introduces how to create and manage a Reach & Frequency campaign. 
	- [TopView](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1804258360285186): Introduces the availability of TopView ads in the API. 
	- [Upgraded Smart+ Campaign](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1853452461203458): Introduces the benefits of Upgraded Smart+ Campaigns.
	- [Dedicated Campaign](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740029173531649): Introduces iOS 14 Dedicated Campaigns and SKAN 4.0 Dedicated Campaigns, the steps to create and retrieve a Dedicated Campaign, and Advanced Dedicated Campaigns.
	- [Super Split Testing](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1742668394531842): Introduces Split testing and its [use cases](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1742668948278274).
	- [Budget](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739381246298114): Introduces supported budget amount, budget modes, and budget type combinations at both campaign and ad group levels.
	- [Promote campaign](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1785880454546433): Introduces the availability, statuses, supported filters, and reporting data for Promote ads.
	- [Realtime API](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1805437148769281): Introduces the steps of enabling Realtime API (RTA) for your ads.
  - [Ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292233615362): At the ad group level, you need to configure placements, target audience, budget and schedule, bidding and more. 
	- [Create an ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738857874837506): Introduces how to create an ad group.
	- [Placements](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292441739265): Introduces placement types and supported placements.
	- [Targeting](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292439329793): Introduces [Audience targeting](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739381236849665) and [Contextual targeting](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292519923713) to help you pinpoint the exact audiences you want your ads to be displayed to.
	- [Brand safety](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739381752981505): Introduces Brand Safety solutions to keep the reputation of a brand safe when they advertise online.
	- [Conversion events](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1739361474981889): Introduces Conversion events enumeration values.
	- [Bidding](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292444424193): Introduces bidding features and Value optimization to help advertisers maximize their ROAS.
	- [Attribution window and event count](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1777694366654465): Introduces the general attribution setting options, including click-through attribution window, view-through attribution window, and event count, as well as the specific supported choices for different advertising objectives.
	- [Supported secondary statuses for a primary status](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1757239620352002): Introduces supported secondary statuses for a certain primary status at the campaign, ad group, and ad levels.
	- [Estimated Delivery Results](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1742668394531842): Introduces Estimated Delivery Results (EDR) to help improve your ad group performance by providing recommended bids or recommended budgets.
  - [Ad](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292235571201): Ad is the content that is presented to the target audience. This may include video or image material, ad text and call-to-action texts.
	- [Create an ad](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292553358338): Introduces how to create an ad.
	- [Smart Creative](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1767322784934914): Introduces how to use Smart Creative.
	- [Identities](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738958351620097): Introduces supported authorized identities and related endpoints.
	- [Deeplink](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1779541971843073): Introduces the deeplink types, fallback type, and the supported deeplink and fallback type settings for specific scenarios.
	- [Custom Product Pages](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1798658439859202): Introduces how to use Custom Product Pages in ads.
	- [AIGC self-disclosure toggle](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1799186631031809): Introduces the supported advertising objectives and ad formats for the AIGC (Artificial Intelligence Generated Content) self-disclosure toggle and how to use the toggle.
	- [Automatic Enhancements](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855450880142402): Introduces how to use Automatic Enhancements in ads.
   - [FAQs](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?rid=7xusz43oh3h&id=1767309272869890): Introduces common questions about Campaign Management API.
