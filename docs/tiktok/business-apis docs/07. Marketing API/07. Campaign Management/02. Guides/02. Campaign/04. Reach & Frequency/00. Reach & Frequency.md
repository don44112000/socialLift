# Reach & Frequency

Reach & Frequency (R&F) is a campaign buying type that helps TikTok For Business advertisers plan and buy campaigns in advance. 

To find more details about R&F ads, see [About Reach & Frequency](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1744571201117186). 

To find out how to create an R&F campaign, see [Create a Reach & Frequency campaign](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1744571205201922).

To find out how to manage an R&F campaign, see [Manage a Reach & Frequency campaign](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1744571207714818). 

To find out R&F API, see [API reference](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1744571210012673).
