# Regular metrics
<strong>Regular metrics are data for regular campaigns (non-iOS 14 Dedicated Campaign).</strong>

> <span style="color: DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>Metrics that have no consumption or are meaningless under the query conditions will be returned as `-` . For example, ad groups under one campaign may have different optimization goals, and query for `result`, `cost_per_result`, `result_rate` (which is based on the optimization goal) at the campaign level is meaningless.

# SKAN metrics
**SKAN metrics are data for SKAN Dedicated Campaigns**. Learn more about [Dedicated Campaign quota](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1740029173531649#item-link-Dedicated%20Campaign%20quota) and [Reporting metrics for Dedicated Campaigns](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1740029173531649#item-link-Reporting%20metrics%20for%20Dedicated%20Campaigns). SKAN metrics support campaign, ad group, and ad levels, and do not support hourly data. 

To determine whether a Dedicated Campaign is a SKAN Dedicated Campaign, use [/campaign/get/](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1739315828649986) and check whether the returned `disable_skan_campaign` is `false`.

> <span style="color: DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>SKAN metrics will only display values for SKAN Dedicated Campaigns. For non-SKAN Dedicated Campaigns and regular campaigns, they will display `0`.
