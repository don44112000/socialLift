# Overview

Understanding community conversations towards your business is essential for effective marketing. Mentions API is a tool for monitoring mentions and analyzing community conversations towards your business on TikTok. By providing insights into community conversations, customer feedback, and social media trends, Mentions API can help you to improve your marketing strategies and build a stronger business presence on TikTok.


# Benefits of Mentions API
- **Gaining programmatic insights into community conversations**: Mentions API enables you to programmatically retrieve relevant posts across TikTok that explicitly mention your business, and top keywords and hashtags that appear in the captions of relevant posts. This allows you to gain valuable insights into community conversations towards your business.
- **Staying up-to-date with customer feedback**: Mentions API enables you to address customer inquiries and complaints timely. By staying up-to-date with customer feedback, you can improve customer satisfaction and build a loyal customer base.
- **Identity opportunities for social media strategy**: Mentions API enables you to stay updated on the latest community conversations towards your business, and identify new opportunities for engagement and create content that resonates with your audience.

# Terms
- Business: An entity that wants to monitor their mentions and analyze community conversations about their business on TikTok.
- Developer: An entity developing applications that utilize the Mentions API to obtain insights.
- Mentioned post: A TikTok post that mentions a business's handle in the post caption. 
- Brand hashtag: A hashtag that matches any of the hashtags configured for your business. You can configure up to 50 hashtags for your business by using [/business/mention/hashtag/add/](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1833831427732481).
