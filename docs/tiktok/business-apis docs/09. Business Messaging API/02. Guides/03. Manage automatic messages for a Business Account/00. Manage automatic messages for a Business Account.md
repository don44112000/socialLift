# Manage automatic messages for a Business Account

This article walks you through the steps to manage automatic messages for a Business Account.  

## Introduction  
Automatic messages are essential tools for creating a seamless communication experience with your audience. They help businesses greet users, answer frequently asked questions, and guide conversations effectively. 

Automatic messages include the following key features:  
- **Welcome messages**: Greet users when they first interact with your Business Account.  
- **Suggested questions**: Provide quick answers to frequently asked questions.  
- **Chat prompts**: Encourage users to initiate conversations with interactive buttons.  

You can use the Business Messaging API to manage these features and create a more engaging and efficient communication experience for your users.  

## Steps
- To learn about how to create a welcome message to greet users and set the tone for your communication, see [Manage the welcome message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184264385538). 
- To learn about how to configure suggested questions to address common inquiries and streamline communication, see [Manage the suggested questions for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184277518338).  
- To learn about how to customize chat prompts to guide users toward specific topics or actions, see [Manage the chat prompts for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184291198977).
