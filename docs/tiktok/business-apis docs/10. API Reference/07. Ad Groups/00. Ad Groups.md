# Ad Groups

Ad groups are the middle level of advertising. You can set specific ad placements, target audiences, budgets, schedule, bids, and optimization goals for each ad group at the ad group level.

See [Guides-Ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1745292233615362) to find out the procedure for creating an ad group and learn about the concepts of targeting, placements, bidding, brand safety, Automatic Creative Optimization, and Split testing. See [Ad structure](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739381193120770) to find out the three advertising levels.

This group consists of the following endpoints.

* [Get ad groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739314558673922)
* [Get the dynamic quota on active ad groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1768463039162369)
* [Estimate audience size](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740302379236353)
* [Create an ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739499616346114)
* [Update an ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739586761631745)
* [Update the statuses of ad groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739591716326402)
* [Update the budgets of ad groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739591133130817)
