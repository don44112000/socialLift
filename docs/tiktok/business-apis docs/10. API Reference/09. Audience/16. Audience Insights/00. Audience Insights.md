# Audience Insights

Audience demographics, location, interests, and devices are estimated based on factors such as user behavior on the app, the information provided by users, and device information. The accuracy of such insights may vary with the information provided. These estimates are not a guarantee of results.
<br>To learn more about its concepts and best practices, visit [Best Practices for Audience Insights](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/audience-insights-best-practices?lang=en) on TikTok Business Help Center. 
><span style="color:DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>At present, we only support endpoints for pre-campaign Audience Insights, which correspond to the 'Potential Audience' feature in the TikTok Ads Manager. We do not support endpoints for post-campaign Audience Insights, corresponding to the 'Reached Audience' feature in the TikTok Ads Manager.

This group consists of the following endpoints: 
- [Get details of potential audiences](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1797016593782785)
- [Get details of audience overlap](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1797023590780930)
