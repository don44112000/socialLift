# BC Management

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. BC Administration API enables you to get a list of Business Centers accounts that a user has access to.

This group consists of the following endpoint:

* [Get Business Centers](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1737115687501826)
* [Get the activity log of a Business Center](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1820767460168705)
