# BC Payments

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. BC Payments API enables you to process a payment for an ad account, and get the balances, transaction records, or cost records of ad accounts or a Business Center.

This group consists of the following endpoints:

* [Process a payment](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939095321601)
* [Get the balance and budget of ad accounts](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939106470913)
* [Get the balance of a BC ](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939128198145)
* [Get the transaction records of a BC or ad accounts](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1792849810925569)
* [Get the transaction records of ad accounts](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939116353538)
* [Get the transaction records of a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939140408322)
* [Get the budget change history of an ad account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1830173979548674)
* [Get the cost records of a BC and ad accounts](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1829079287639041)
