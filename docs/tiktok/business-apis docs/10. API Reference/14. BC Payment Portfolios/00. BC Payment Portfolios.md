# BC Payment Portfolios

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. Payment Portfolio is a one stop workspace that allows you to view and and manage your options for making payments, including credit lines, cash balance, ad credit, and payment methods, in a unified manner across TikTok for Business platforms. To learn more about Payment Portfolio, see [About Payment Portfolio](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/about-payment-portfolio).

> <span style="color:DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>
> You can only use BC Payment Portfolios API when you have enabled the multiple Payment Portfolios feature. Multiple Payment Portfolios for one client is currently an allowlist-only feature. If you would like to access it, please contact your TikTok representative.

BC Payment Portfolios API enables you to:
- Retrieve existing Payment Portfolios
- Create a new Payment Portfolio
- Link ad accounts to a Payment Portfolio or retrieve linked ad accounts
- Allocate the credit line to Payment Portfolios
- Retrieve authorized users who have access to a Payment Portfolio

This group consists of the following endpoints:
- [Get Payment Portfolios](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839151931301954)
- [Create a Payment Portfolio](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839151986039809)
- [Link ad accounts to a Payment Portfolio](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839151992566081)
- [Allocate the credit line to Payment Portfolios](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839151998450754)
- [Get ad accounts linked to a Payment Portfolio](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839152005599234)
- [Get authorized users for a Payment Portfolio](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839152031645121)
