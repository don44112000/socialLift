# BC Assets

Business Center (BC) is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. BC Assets API enables you to manage assets in a Business Center.

This group consists of the following endpoints: 
* [Create an ad account](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939020318721)
* [Update an ad account](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939050770434)
* [Disable an ad account](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1752349244331009)
* [Upload a business certificate](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739938996913218)
* [Get qualifications within a Business Center](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1770118680584194)
* [Check the UnionPay verification requirement for a business license](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1813772238056449)
* [Submit UnionPay verification for a business license](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1813772274039809)
* [Create an Organization Account in a Business Center](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855027199571138)
* [Get assets](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739432717798401)
* [Get assets as admin](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739433007779841)
* [Assign an asset](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739438211077121)
* [Unassign an asset](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739448126749698)
* [Obtain a TikTok account ad delivery authorization URL](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1846868897541122)
* [Link a TikTok account to an ad account in Business Center](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1846868953025538)
* [Unlink a TikTok account from an ad account in Business Center](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855027260369921)
* [Get ad accounts linked to a TikTok account in Business Center](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855027294743554)
* [Manage a pixel](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739659520972802) 
	- [Transfer a pixel from advertiser to BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1753994928261122)
	- [Link a pixel to ad accounts / Unlink](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1753994950327297)
	- [Get ad accounts linked to a pixel](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1753994966965249)
* [Get partners by an asset](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739663268166722)
* [Get members by an asset](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739659558931458)
* [Delete assets from a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739657251102722)
* [Get binding info of an asset](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739659584022529)
