# BC Billing Groups

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. A Billing Group is a group of ad accounts that have the same invoicing and payment settings. BC Billing Groups API enables you to manage Billing Groups in a Business Center.

This group consists of the following endpoints: 
* [Create a Billing Grroup](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738947780033537)
* [Update a Billing Group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738947843447809)
* [Get Billing Groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738944324279297)
* [Get the advertiser list of a Billing Group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738458108705794)
