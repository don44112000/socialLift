# BC Partners

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. BC Partners API enables you to manage partners and the assets that you have shared with a partner in a Business Center.

This group consists of the following endpoints: 
* [Get the partners of a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739662727395330)
* [Add a partner to a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739662756510721)
* [Delete a partner from a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739662779301890)
* [Cancel the sharing of assets](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739662800930817)
* [Get the assets of a partner](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739662828320769)
