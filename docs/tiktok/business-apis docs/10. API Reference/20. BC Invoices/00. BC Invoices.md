# BC Invoices

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok.  BC Invoices API enables you to manage invoices in a Business Center.

This group consists of the following endpoints:
* [Get the invoices of a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740295890332674)
* [Get the unpaid amount of a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740295904843777)
* [Download the invoices of a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740295923474433)
	- [Download synchronously](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754000198430722)
	- [Download asynchronously](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754000224141313)
	 	- [Create an asynchronous download task](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754000448122882)
		- [Get asynchronous download tasks](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754000480307265)
		- [Get asynchronous download task list](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754000504437762)
