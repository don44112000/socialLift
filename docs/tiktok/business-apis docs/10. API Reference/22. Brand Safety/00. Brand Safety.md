# Brand Safety

Brand Safety solutions are designed to keep the reputation of a brand safe when they advertise online. The Brand Safety Hub is a centralized location at the ad account level where you can configure your first-party brand safety preferences. These settings will automatically apply to future campaigns. Brand Safety API enables you to set, update, or retrieve the Brand Safety Hub settings of an ad account.

> <span style="color:DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>
>Currently, the settings configured through Brand Safety API will automatically apply to your future [Smart+ Campaigns](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1767959274087425) but will not apply to future regular campaigns created using [/campaign/create/](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1739318962329602).

To learn about available brand safety features, see [Brand safety](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1739381752981505).

This group consists of the following endpoints:
- [Get the Brand Safety Hub settings of an ad account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1830112550443073)
- [Set or update the Brand Safety Hub settings of an ad account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1830112569774274)
