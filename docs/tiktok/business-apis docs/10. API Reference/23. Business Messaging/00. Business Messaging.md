# Business Messaging

Messaging is an integral part of how businesses manage their presence as well as doing business with users on social media platforms. Business Messaging API is a suite of interfaces enabling authorized Business Accounts to exchange direct messages with TikTok users through API-integrated platforms.

You can see [Guides](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184100272338) to learn more about the authorization and authentication procedure, webhook schema, and more.

This group consists of the following endpoints:

**Direct messages**

- [Send a message to a conversation](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184403754242)
- [Get a list of conversations](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184415059970)
- [Get a list of messages](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184425841170)
- [Upload an image](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184437720193)
- [Download an image from a message](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184455450626)
- [Enable or disable Comment-to-Message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1843697551083522)
- [Get the Comment-to-Message setting of a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1843697576682497)

**Webhooks**

- [Create a Business Messaging Webhook configuration](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184470423554)
- [Get a Business Messaging Webhook configuration](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184488625234)
- [Delete a Business Messaging Webhook configuration](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184497985537)

**Automatic messages**

- [Create an automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184584869890)
- [Update the automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184596566017)
- [Turn on or turn off an automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184607786242)
- [Get the automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184619749377)
- [Delete the automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184630106386)
- [Sort the automatic message for a Business Account](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832184641212433)
