# Leads

Lead generation is the process of identifying and cultivating potential customers for a business's products or services. This is done through a prospective client submitting information to a business to be contacted at a later time or date to make the final sale. Leads API enables you to perform end-to-end testing for lead generation, download leads, subscribe to leads, migrate leads and get the fields of an Instant Form.

See [Lead generation](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739289524101122) to learn about how to download leads and create and test a subscription.

This group consists of the following endpoints:

**Testing leads**
* [Create a test lead](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739047132614722)
* [Get a test lead](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739048460493826)
* [Delete a test lead](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739049313198081)

**Downloading leads**
* [Create a lead download task](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739052936772610)
* [Download leads](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739053563877378)

**Subscribing to leads**
* [Create a subscription](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739092028876801)
* [Get subscription details](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739093832125442)
* [Cancel a subscription](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739094758789122)

**Leads migration**
* [Migrate leads to a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740029931324417)
* [Get form libraries](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740029915327489)

**Get fields or leads**
* [Get the fields of an Instant Form](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739054080455681)
* [Get fields of an Instant Form or direct message leads](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832112377641026)
* [Get an Instant Form lead or a direct message lead](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1832112833212434)
