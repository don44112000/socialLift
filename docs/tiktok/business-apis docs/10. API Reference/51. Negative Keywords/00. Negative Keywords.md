# Negative Keywords

Negative keywords are a feature that helps you optimize Search Ads delivery by filtering out irrelevant queries or queries that may affect your brand safety. If the user's search term matches a negative keyword you have created for an ad group or a campaign, your ad under the ad group or campaign won't be shown.


This group consists of the following endpoints:
- [Get negative keywords](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775104887052289)
- [Create negative keywords](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775104895291393)
- [Update a negative keyword](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775104902283266)
- [Delete negative keywords](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775104910010369)
- [Download negative keywords](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775104916876290)
