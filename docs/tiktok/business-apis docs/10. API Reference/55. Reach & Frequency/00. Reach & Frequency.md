# Reach & Frequency

Reach & Frequency (R&F) is a campaign buying type that helps TikTok For Business advertisers plan and buy campaigns in advance. To find out more details and how to create an R&F campaign, see [here](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739470723315713). 

> <span style="color: DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>The Reach and Frequency feature is in GA. Developers can build integrations with this feature. However, the integations would only work if the ad accounts are allowlisted for this feature. To have your ad accounts allowlisted, please reach out to your TikTok representative.

This group consists of the following endpoints:
* [Get inventory estimates](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738235487941633)
* [Create an R&F ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738235338194945)
* [Update an R&F ad group](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738235402874882)
* [Cancel the R&F ad order](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740489099367425)
* [Get estimated info of R&F ad groups](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740489551354881)
* [Query contracts](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740489855138818)
* [Get R&F time zones](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740490147129345)
