# Industries

Industry information can be used in the following scenarios:
- To set up an ad account in a Business Center via [/bc/advertiser/create/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939020318721), you need to specify via `industry` the industry or industries the ad account belongs to.
- To get Smart Text recommendations via [/creative/smart_text/generate/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739084248002626), you can provide `industry_id` to generate Smart Text recommendations specific to that industry.

# Industry IDs for ad accounts created in a Business Center
The industry IDs available for ad accounts created in a Business Center vary depending on the Business Center type. Refer to the tables below to learn about the available industry IDs for ad accounts created in self-serve Business Centers and non-self-serve Business Centers. 

To determine whether your Business Center is self-serve or non-self-serve, check the ` type` field returned in the [/bc/get/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1737115687501826) endpoint:
- If `type` is `SELF_SERVICE` or `SELF_SERVICE_AGENCY`, the Business Center is self-serve.
- If `type` is `AGENCY` or `DIRECT`, the Business Center is non-self-serve.

> <span style="color:DodgerBlue">**Note**</span><br>
> To create an ad account in a Business Center using [/bc/advertiser/create/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739939020318721), you need to specify a Tier 2 industry ID via the request parameter `industry`.
