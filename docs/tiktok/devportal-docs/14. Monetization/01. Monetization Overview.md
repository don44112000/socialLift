# Monetization Overview

TikTok allows you to generate revenue by integrating monetization features into your mini game. The methods available are designed to fit different product needs and business models: 
- Set up in-app ads to drive traffic and generate revenue, allowing users to earn rewards in return
- Offer subscriptions and one-time purchases for premium content and upgrades 

## Prerequisites

To access monetization features, your business must be verified by TikTok for Developers. Learn more about [verifying your business](/doc/verify-your-business). 
## Set up and manage monetization 

Monetization settings can be accessed on the organization level within the Developer Portal. 

**Note**: Only registered organization admins can access the Monetization page.
1. Go to **My organizations**, then click your organization.
1. Click the **Monetization** tab.

On the **Monetization **page, you can enable features, view revenue statements, and manage payouts. 
- **Overview**: Set up monetization by enabling features and registering a payout account.
  - Sign contracts to enable in-app ads and in-app purchases for mini games
  - Provide TikTok with business and tax information to set up your payout account. 

- **Revenue**: Manage and confirm statements for revenue settlement. Learn more about settling revenue statements. 
- **Payouts**: Submit invoices to get paid for your settled income. Learn more about managing payouts. 

## Monetization features

TikTok offers these monetization features for your mini app: 
- **In-App Ads: **Allow users to watch ads and earn rewards in return (rewarded ads and interstitial ads) 
- **In-App Purchases: **Allow users to buy premium content and upgrades (one-time purchases or subscriptions)

## Monetization process

The complete workflow for monetization, from setting up monetization features to receiving payouts, requires multiple steps on the Developer Portal and technical integrations for your mini app. 

**Note**: [Business verification](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/verify-your-business) is a prerequisite to access monetization features and must be completed first.
### Pay in

Pay in setup is the process by which you enable and configure monetization features for your mini app, so you can generate revenue once it is launched.
1. Sign contracts to [enable monetization features](/doc/enable-monetization-features)
1. Integrate TikTok's monetization APIs for [IAA](/doc/in-app-ads) and [IAP](/doc/in-app-purchases) with your mini game, test and debug
1. Start earning after launching your mini game, track monetization metrics and data

### Pay out

Pay out setup lets you receive payments after submitting financial paperwork.
1. Submit business and tax information and set up payout accounts to receive payments
1. Confirm revenue statements
1. Submit invoices to receive payment for settled income

To get started, [enable monetization features](/doc/enable-monetization-features) on the Developer Portal.
