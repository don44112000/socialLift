# Compliance Enforcement

Members of your organization can monitor and manage your app's compliance with TikTok’s safety policies and community guidelines. Use this feature to view specific policy violations, understand their corresponding enforcement actions, and take direct actions like submitting disputes or resubmitting your app for review after issues are resolved. 
## Compliance tab

The compliance tab exists on the organization level in the Developer Portal. To navigate to the compliance dashboard: 
1. Go to your organization in the **Developer Portal**.
1. Click the **Compliance** tab.

### Manage violations

If your app has violations, you must review and resolve them to maintain compliance. 
<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/b14615431a4da7d486f98ecaa89979e5" alt="image" width="2082px" />

There are two types of enforcement actions that may be triggered against your app, depending on your violation: 
- **Warning**: For experience violations, you must fix or appeal the violation by the stated deadline or your app will be taken offline. The deadline is set 30 days after the violation was first detected.
- **App taken offline**: For content security violations, your app is immediately taken offline and is no longer visible to users. You must fix the violation within your app and resubmit the new app version for review. 

If your app was taken offline or you fail to resolve your violation by the stated deadline, your production version will show as blocked on your app's **Code version** page. 
<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/7ef7517c33287c195d8ece328a05e640" alt="image" width="2084px" />

Depending on your scenario, you may need to submit a new code version for review to relaunch the mini app on TikTok, or relaunch the app directly. 
<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/06321397aafeb9475cd87c232781f1c1" alt="image" width="1280px" />

**Note**: You’ll also receive inbox and email notifications when warnings, enforcement actions, or dispute results are issued.

Your mini app may have been flagged for any of the following policy violations: 
- Adult nudity and sexual activities
- Game content and functionality
- Harassment and bullying
- Hateful behavior
- Illegal activities and regulated goods
- Integrity and authenticity
- Minor safety
- Required capability
- Suicide, self-harm, and dangerous acts
- Violent and graphic content
- Violent extremism

#### Fix violation in app

To resolve a violation in app, you must fix it directly in your app's code package. 
1. Click the **Take action** dropdown next to the violation.
1. Select **Fix in app**.

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/647e87578a0419dcce75f52001abb2bf" alt="image" width="1280px" />

This navigates you to your app's **Code version** page. 
<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/bd78bbd2cc3811d9aff36a152280d51b" alt="image" width="1280px" />

You must fix the issue within your app's code package, then resubmit it for review to release it as a new version. For information on what policy was violated, refer to the [TikTok Minis content requirements](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-content-requirements). 
1. Once the violation is fixed within the code package, click the **Add code asset** button.
1. Select the code asset type: **HTML** or **Native**. 
1. Upload your code package. 
1. Add a note indicating which violation has been fixed.
1. Submit the uploaded code asset for review. 

For more information about the release process for your mini app, consult the following guides: 
- [Release Your Mini Game](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/submit-your-app-for-review?enter_method=left_navigation)
- [Release Your Mini Drama](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app)

#### Appeal violation

You may appeal a violation by submitting supporting documents and additional comments. You may appeal each violation only once. 
1. Click the **Take action** dropdown.
1. Select **Appeal**.
1. Submit supporting documentation to appeal your violation and write additional details that may support your case. You may appeal each violation only once.
  1. JPEG and PNG
  1. Maximum 10 files, up to 10 MB each

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/38fb1bc985bce0ccab8c9937abaa41f4" alt="image" width="1280px" />

Once received, TikTok will review your case within 1-3 business days. 
- If your appeal was successful, you can relaunch the appealed app version directly. 
- If your appeal was unsuccessful, you may no longer use the appealed app version. You must submit a new app version for review to relaunch your app. For more information about the release process for your mini app, consult the following guides: 
  - [Release Your Mini Game](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/submit-your-app-for-review?enter_method=left_navigation)
  - [Release Your Mini Drama](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app)

## Content requirements

The content of your mini app must abide by the stated content requirements for TikTok Minis. View the full list of [content requirements and guidelines](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-content-requirements).
