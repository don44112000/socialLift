# Prepare Product Content

This guide demonstrates how to configure your products. There are two methods to create and manage product content: via the Merchant Portal, or SaaS APIs.
## Create a product via Merchant Portal

1. Log into the Merchant Portal.
1. Click on the **Products** tab.
1. Click the **Create Product** button.

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/e4f85794f2bb8ed76b68623c5b5a292d" alt="image" width="2996px" />

1. When prompted to create a new product, select the relevant category and type. **Discount voucher** will be selected by default. Click the **Next** button.

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/feba0d43a460b0cbd6c69a9b8902fa19" alt="image" width="2560px" />

1. Upload an image of the product and input the product's name. Click the **Confirm **button. 

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/d34d1560375087dc557511696fdc6390" alt="image" width="2560px" />

1. The newly created product will be listed with the status **Under review**.

<img src="https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/tiktok-open-platform-sg/ttop_doc_images/b199aa68ecce560a33485b914829630c" alt="image" width="2428px" />

You can view, edit, withdraw, or offline each product using the icons next to the listed product.
