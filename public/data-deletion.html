Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-04-19

If you want SocialLift to delete the data associated with your Facebook account (tokens, cached Page metadata, logs), follow the steps below.

Option 1 — Revoke from Facebook

  1. Open Facebook → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Go to Apps and Websites.
  3. Find SocialLift under Active.
  4. Click Remove.

Facebook will notify us via a Deauthorize callback and we will delete your stored tokens and cached Page data within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email request

Email omlokhande112000@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Facebook account with the subject line "Delete my data". Include your Facebook user ID if known. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

What we delete

  • User and Page access tokens
  • Cached user, Page, and Instagram business account metadata
  • Scheduled posts and drafts you created in SocialLift
  • Request logs linked to your user ID

What we keep

Aggregated, de-identified usage statistics that cannot be used to re-identify you may be retained. Content published to your Pages or Instagram accounts remains on those platforms and is managed by Meta.
