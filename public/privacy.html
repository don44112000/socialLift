Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-19

SocialLift (“we”, “the Service”) helps businesses publish content and manage messages on Facebook and Instagram through the Meta Graph API. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Facebook account data: your name, Facebook user ID, profile picture, and email (only if you grant it).
  • Page and Instagram data: the list of Pages you manage, Page IDs, Page access tokens, connected Instagram business account IDs, and content metadata for the features you use (posts, messages, insights).
  • Usage data: basic request logs (timestamps, IP) for abuse prevention and debugging. Retained up to 30 days.

How we use it

  • To publish, read, and manage content on the Pages and Instagram accounts you authorize.
  • To send and receive messages on your behalf through Messenger and Instagram Direct.
  • To surface analytics in your SocialLift dashboard.

How we store it

Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest and are only decrypted in-memory to make authorized Graph API calls. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Sharing

We share data only with Meta (to execute API calls you triggered) and with sub-processors strictly necessary to operate the Service (hosting, database). We do not share with advertisers or data brokers.

Your rights

  • You can revoke SocialLift at any time from Facebook → Settings → Business Integrations.
  • You can request deletion of your data — see our Data Deletion page.
  • You can export your data by emailing the contact below.
